The Philosophy

Music deserves
perfection.

Lossify escapes the modern era of compressed streaming and invasive ad-networks. It's an open-source sanctuary built specifically to deliver local music files in absolute bit-perfect quality.

No subscriptions. No data harvesting. No distractions. Just a beautifully crafted interface that gets out of your way and lets you hear your music the way it was meant to be heard.

Capabilities

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

True Lossless Architecture
Native support for FLAC, ALAC, WAV, and AIFF. The audio engine bypasses OS upsampling to ensure every single bit is delivered identically to the original studio master.
Absolute Privacy
Zero telemetry. Zero tracking. Your listening habits remain exclusively on your device.
Adaptive UI
Dynamic theming that adapts to your album art. Every screen feels tailor-made for the music you're playing.
Offline Support
Your local library, always available. No Wi-Fi needed, no streaming dependencies.
Time-Synced Lyrics
Follow along word-for-word with rich, offline-supported LRC integration.
Global Radio
Instantly tune into over 30,000 global internet radio stations built directly into the core player.
AOTY Integration
Album of the Year charts and ratings baked in. Always know what the world is listening to.
Fullscreen Mode
Immersive, distraction-free playback. Album art fills the screen. Nothing else matters.
