## What is Lossify?

Lossify is a music player created out of a genuine love for music. No ads, no algorithmic tracking, no cluttered interfaces — just you and your music in a beautiful, responsive space that puts the listening experience first.

## Features

| | Feature | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 🎵 | **Lossless Audio** | FLAC playback — hear every detail exactly as the artist intended |
| 🔒 | **True Privacy** | No tracking, no data selling. Your listening habits are yours alone |
| 🎨 | **Adaptive UI** | Frosted-glass interface that pulls colors from your album art |
| 📥 | **Offline Support** | Download tracks to your device for listening without internet |
| 📱 | **Cross-Device Sync** | Your library and playlists follow you across all your devices |
| 🎤 | **Synced Lyrics** | Perfectly timed scrolling lyrics that keep up with the music |
| 📡 | **Infinite Radio** | Personalized radio seeded from your taste |
| 🏆 | **AOTY Integration** | Critic and user scores from Album of the Year, right inside the app |
| 🎧 | **Fullscreen Mode** | A cinematic, immersive player for focused listening sessions |
